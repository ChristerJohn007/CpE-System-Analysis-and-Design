Cosmetics Dentistry

The objective of any cosmetic dentistry treatment is twofold.

First, the dentist and patient work together to achieve the desired physical outcome. For instance, patients with missing teeth may require dental implants to complete their smile. Alternatively, they may wish to consider dental bridges as a non-surgical option.

Secondly, the patient’s long-term functionality is always considered. A great smile is even better when the teeth function well, too. The doctors will ensure that every cosmetic dentistry procedure they perform maintains or improves patient function.
The end result is an incredible smile that meets the patient’s aesthetic and practical dental needs.

Benefits of Cosmetic Dental Procedures

Not sure if cosmetic dentistry is the right decision? Some of the advantages to choosing cosmetic dentistry include:

  • Enhanced aesthetics
  • Improved confidence when smiling
  • Improved oral function
  • Less worry about dental health
  • A healthier, more youthful-looking smile

A Wide Range of Cosmetic Dentistry Services

At Mateo Dental Clinic, we offer several types of cosmetic dentistry procedures. In some cases, patients may opt to undergo multiple procedures at once to achieve optimal, comprehensive results.

Some of the more commonly requested cosmetic solutions are:

  • Dental bridges
  • Dental crowns
  • Dental implants
  • Porcelain inlays and onlays
  • Porcelain veneers
  • Teeth whitening
